Privacy Policy

Facebook & Instagram Application Integration

Last updated: October 22, 2025
Platform Coverage: This privacy policy covers our application's integration with both Facebook and Instagram platforms. We use Facebook for Messenger interactions and Instagram for content and messaging features.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Facebook and Instagram application, we may collect the following information:

Facebook Profile Information:

  • Public profile information including name, profile picture, and Facebook ID
  • Email address associated with your Facebook account
  • Facebook Pages you manage (if applicable)
  • Facebook Messenger conversations and message content
  • Facebook Page insights and analytics data

Instagram Profile Information:

  • Instagram username, profile picture, and user ID
  • Instagram media content (photos, videos, captions)
  • Instagram Stories and highlights
  • Instagram Direct Messages (when authorized)
  • Instagram account insights and engagement metrics
  • Instagram business profile information
  • Hashtags and location data from your posts

General Usage Data:

  • Information about how you interact with our application
  • Device information for security and functionality purposes
  • IP address and browser information
  • Application usage patterns and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Facebook Data Usage:

  • Enable Messenger chatbot functionality and automated responses
  • Manage and post content to your Facebook Pages
  • Provide analytics and insights for your Facebook presence
  • Facilitate customer service and support interactions

Instagram Data Usage:

  • Display and manage your Instagram content within our application
  • Enable automated posting and content scheduling
  • Provide Instagram analytics and performance metrics
  • Facilitate Instagram Direct Message automation
  • Enable content curation and social media management

General Purposes:

  • Authenticate and verify your identity across platforms
  • Personalize your experience within the application
  • Improve our application's functionality and user experience
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our services
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance

3. Platform-Specific Permissions

Facebook Permissions We Request:

  • pages_messaging: Send and receive messages on behalf of your Pages
  • pages_manage_metadata: Manage your Page settings and information
  • pages_read_engagement: Read engagement data from your Pages
  • pages_manage_posts: Create, edit, and delete posts on your Pages
  • public_profile: Access your basic profile information

Instagram Permissions We Request:

  • instagram_basic: Access basic profile information and media
  • instagram_content_publish: Publish content to your Instagram account
  • instagram_manage_messages: Send and receive Instagram Direct Messages
  • instagram_manage_insights: Access Instagram analytics and insights
  • user_profile: Read your Instagram profile information
  • user_media: Read your Instagram media content

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who assist in application operation (hosting, analytics, etc.)
  • Meta Platforms: Data is processed in compliance with Facebook and Instagram API terms
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between our application and Meta's servers is encrypted
  • Access Tokens: Secure handling and storage of Facebook and Instagram access tokens
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and compliance reviews
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization protocols
  • Data Isolation: Facebook and Instagram data are processed separately with appropriate controls

6. Data Retention

We retain your information according to the following schedule:

  • Profile Data: Retained while your account is active or as needed for services
  • Message Content: Facebook Messenger and Instagram DM data retained for up to 90 days
  • Media Content: Instagram photos/videos cached temporarily for display purposes
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated insights retained for up to 2 years for trend analysis
  • Usage Logs: Application usage data retained for up to 1 year for improvement purposes

7. Your Rights and Choices

Universal Rights:

  • Access: Request access to all personal information we have about you
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

Facebook Specific Rights:

  • Revoke Facebook app permissions through Facebook Settings
  • Control Facebook Page access and messaging permissions
  • Manage Facebook data downloads through Facebook's tool

Instagram Specific Rights:

  • Revoke Instagram app access through Instagram Settings
  • Control Instagram content sharing and posting permissions
  • Manage Instagram business account data access

Instagram Platform Policy Compliance:
